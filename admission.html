ADMISSION

Admission to Plus one classes in Govt and aided Higher Secondary Schools is done through Centralised Allotment Process called Single Window Admission(Ernakulam). This allotment Process starts immediately after the publication of SSLC/10th standard results. Admission through this system is to procure the merit seats in Govt and aided Higher secondary schools.
This is an endeavor to clear the doubts regarding plus one admission that are likely to arise among the students and parents.   To get admission to St.Francis HSS For Girls, Aluva (Aided), kindly use the Single Window Admission (Ekajalakam) website http://www.hscap.kerala.gov.in. The school code is 7072 and the name of the school is St.Francis HSS For Girls, Aluva. Feel free to contact our school to clarify any doubts regarding admission; contact number: 0484 2625850.
