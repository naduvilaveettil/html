MESSAGE FROM PRINCIPAL

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world”
– Nelson Mandela

Yes, Education is a process of receiving or giving a systematic instruction, especially in a school or university. In this pandemic situation, it becomes a process of strengthening or imparting optimistic thoughts and measures to look at the brighter side of our lives. St. Francis HSS for Girls, Aluva stands for such an education for girls, which in turn, builds up a better society. Servant of God Mother Eliswa Vakayil, the foundress of this Congregation of Teresian Carmelites (CTC) instituted this Congregation in 1866 with the foresighted vision of empowering women to safeguard the values of the society. Here we provide all the essential facilities to become a better person, as we are aware that ‘Education is not preparation for life, education is life itself”. The students passed out of our institution keep in contact with us which reveals t us that “Education is what remains after one has forgotten what one has learned in school”. Yes, our school is a home far from home. St. Francis HSS for Girls, Aluva creates a nostalgic feeling for all who are in contact with us. “All for the glory of God!!”
